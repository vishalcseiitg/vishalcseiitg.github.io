ABOUT



I’m a PhD student at IIT Roorkee (Department of Computer Science & Engineering). Photography is my creative space — I’m learning, exploring, and sharing the moments and details that catch my eye.


My work leans into light, geometry, and everyday scenes turned into simple visual stories.


Collaborations, photo-walks, and creative projects are welcome.
