Grant Application


There are organizations that provide funding in the form of grants for various purposes. Examples are scholarships, doctoral grants, funding for research and/or development projects, co-payments for presentations at conferences, among others.

These funds are generally difficult to obtain, and require either the submission of a detailed and structured research/development project, or in the case of scholarships, require excellent academic performance.

Whatever the case, it is always important:

  • Carefully check the eligibility and selection criteria
  • Correctly follow the instructions of the submission process
  • Pay attention to the required formats and technical details
  • Attach all requested documents and important information for the process
  • Know well the terms and obligations of the grant
  • Follow deadlines
  • Check the need for updates
